Privacy

Privacy notice.

Last updated 15 May 2026.

What we collect

When you write to Aldridge & Charles Marine through this site, we receive the name and email address you provide, the message you compose, and, where you submit a request about a specific vessel, the slug of that listing. Server logs maintained by our hosting provider include your IP address, browser identifier, and request time for a short retention window, used for security and traffic monitoring only.

How we use it

Enquiries are read by the director on duty and responded to within two working days. We use the information to answer your message and, where appropriate, to begin a confidential conversation about your vessel. We do not sell, rent, or barter any information you provide.

Retention

Enquiries are kept until the matter is closed or you request deletion. Server logs are kept for ninety days unless required longer for security investigation. Client correspondence under retainer is kept for the duration of the engagement and seven years thereafter, in line with maritime professional conventions.

Third parties

Site infrastructure is hosted on Cloudflare. Email replies are sent from our own mailbox at office@acmarine.com. No marketing automation, no advertising trackers, no third-party analytics scripts run on this site.

Your rights

You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, or its deletion, at any time, by writing to office@acmarine.com. We will respond within twenty working days.
